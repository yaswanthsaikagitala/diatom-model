These are subsets of the “UDE DIATOMS in the Wild 2024” dataset containing only those diatom species which are represented by at least 25, 50 or 100 examples each. 

More detailed information can be found in the accompanying publication:
Venkataramanan, Kloster, Burfeid-Castellanos et al. (2024): “UDE DIATOMS in the Wild 2024”: An new image dataset of freshwater diatoms for training deep learning models.
